On Reflecting

January 1, 2026

Another year goes by.

I don't know if I'm wiser. Or dumb. This year was different. I cut off people, projects, ideas. I felt lost most of the year even knowing what to do. Metrics prove it.

Every year, people share their highlights and goals. Most of them aren't even started. Last year everyone was crazy about Atomic Habits. Did they achieve their goals this year? I hope so.

Marcus Aurelius said: "Get busy with life's purpose." I don't know what it is but I'm sure is not about worrying about what other people think. So, just do the boring thing once for all. Start writing, reading, walking, breathing. I'm missing something? Who cares. C'est la vie.

Of course I want things for the future. I want a family, great material stuff, play better chess and visit more often the mountains. But what do I want for today? It's a new year. Family prepare coffee. Today I want to be more like a kid. Laugh, play, imagine, believe. Tomorrow I could be an adult again. But today I'm going to be just me.
